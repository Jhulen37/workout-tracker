About Gym Genie

Gym Genie is a revolutionary fitness application designed to help users achieve their fitness goals through personalized workout plans, nutrition advice, and progress tracking. With a vast library of exercises, meal plans, and expert guidance, Gym Genie caters to all fitness levels, from beginners to advanced athletes.

Features

  • Customized workout plans
  • Nutrition and meal planning
  • Progress tracking and analytics
  • Goal setting and achievement tracking
  • Expert advice and community support
